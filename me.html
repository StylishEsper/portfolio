About Me

Hello, my name is Ahmed and I'm a programmer. I am based in Ontario, Canada. I've been programming since 2019. I recently graduated from college with a 3-year advanced diploma in Computer Programming and Analysis. I know a variety of programming languages (mostly OOP languages). Just to list some: C#, C++, COBOL, Java, VB, PHP, and HTML5. I have other skills such as database management (SQL), web development, QA, UI design, 2D art, and SEO.

I grew up playing video games (and I still do), so it has become a passion of mine. After I developed around 3 games without the use of a game engine, I moved on to Unity. I picked it up pretty quickly since I had prior game development knowledge, and I've been developing games with it since.

I spend most of my spare time developing games. It's a fun hobby of mine. I enjoy web and software development as well. I used to work at a small web development firm known as AllAboutWebServices. I would update, build, and manage websites for clients as per their requirements. Currently, I offer freelance game development services on Fiverr. Click here to check it out! Anyway, thanks for reading!
